## Reflection

I tested the recommender with three different profiles: High-Energy Afrobeats, Chill Lofi, and a Conflicting Profile.

The Afrobeats profile returned energetic and happy songs, which made sense because the system strongly rewards genre and mood matches.

The Chill Lofi profile produced calmer, more acoustic songs, showing that energy and acoustic preferences are working correctly.

The Conflicting Profile gave mixed results. Even though the user selected a sad mood, the system mostly prioritized high energy and genre. This shows that some features, like mood, are weaker than others in the scoring system.

Overall, the system works well for clear preferences but struggles when preferences conflict.
